## About me:
I am a NLP/ML researcher, and a mechanical engineer turned electrical engineer turned computer scientist (I know, a long way to get here). 
I obtained both of my undergraduate and master's degrees at the University of Toronto, 
and my master's research was focused on representation learning and computer vision under 
 the supervision of Prof. Brendan Frey ([PSI Lab](https://psi.toronto.edu/)).
 
I have been working in the industry for many years in the domain of NLP. I worked at Canadian start-ups like Meta (acquired by CZI) and Wattpad (acquired by NAVER), 
and big companies like Spotify/ServiceNow (my [CV](cv.pdf)).
I am lucky to have the opportunity going back to school through the [ELLIS Ph.D. Program](https://ellis.eu/). 
I will be joining [TU Darmstadt](https://www.informatik.tu-darmstadt.de/fb20/index.en.jsp) as a Ph.D. student in fall 2021, 
under the supervision of Prof. Iryna Gurevych at the [UKP Lab](https://www.informatik.tu-darmstadt.de/ukp/ukp_home/index.en.jsp) and 
exchange at the University of Cambridge under [Prof. Anna Korhonen](https://sites.google.com/site/annakorhonen/). 

Currently, my research interests include:
multi-modal, multi-lingual, meta-learning / RL with NLP.
